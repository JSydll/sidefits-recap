Meet the future you



Developer (Web Tech, Titanium)

As we believe new technologies can help us solving many of the issues arising on the way of any athlete, we need creative minds to build them. Besides a tech background in web programming and the Appcelerator Titanium framework, thiss requires an interest in all the different aspects of a product made for people - not for functionalities only. The roles in a startup are not as sharply defined as in bigger companies, which leads to more responsibility on the one hand and to a lot more freedom on the other. By working very close together, you get insights on the whole process and different perspective on a technical project. If you love solving ambitious tasks and want to built a game changer in sports experiences, we're happy to get to know you!


Sports Scientist (Performance Measurement, Diagnostics)

Before you can tell whether a new direction takes you to the right spot, you'll have to be able to measure your progress and where exactly you want to go. Speaking about sports, on implication for that can be to provide everyone with easy means to track their performance and progress in sports - no matter on which part of the way they currently on.
We search for sports scientists (post graduate or thesis) interested in the field of performance measurement and diagnostics. You should be passionate about our goal to improve the sports experience for everyone on the long term - by contributing new ideas, by keeping Sidefits near to the ones it's made for or by pushing forward the current features. In turn, you can experience responsibility, the dynamic work processes in a fast evolving business and teamwork par excellence.
