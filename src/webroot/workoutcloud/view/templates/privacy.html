I General

The concept of the Sidefits online platform relies to a great extent on the publication of information regarding individual users to other users by making them accessible via the Sidefits web application. All users can freely access the public profile information (picture, name), training information (created and liked workouts) and network information (followings and followers) of all other users. Sidefits website users do not have the option to change that. By using the website, users explicitly consent that other users may see the information named above. If users do not want other users to get access to their information, they have to leave their profile empty and they have to make sure to not create or like any workouts. If they accidentally do so, they have to delete them in their profile. Email addresses will not be shared with any other user. They may, however, be automatically added to the Sidefits mailing lists. From time to time Sidefits may email you electronic newsletters, announcements, surveys or other information. If you prefer not to receive any or all of these communications, you may opt out by following the directions provided within the electronic newsletters and announcements.

II Types of personally identifiable information collected by Sidefits

Sidefits collects different types of personally identifiable information from their users upon registration and in connection with the usage of the Sidefits website. The personally identifiable information collected by Sidefits belongs to the following categories.
- Profile information which users can freely enter, change and delete via settings such as: first name and last name, gender
- Training information that will be stored when users save workouts or like workouts
- Network information that are generated when users are following or are being followed by other users such as: followers, followings
- Email addresses
Personal identifiable information shall be saved and used in particular to offer new applications or services to the members, to improve such and adapt them to the users needs.

III Use and sharing of personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information with third parties

By registering, the user explicitly agrees that Sidefits shall have the right to use all automatically collected personally identifiable information for purposes of the Sidefits application and future web services. Sidefits does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer users personally identifiable information (see section II above) to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our application and conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. Sidefits may also release users' information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety. Sidefits may also pass personal identifiable information to third parties if the user explicitly gives permission. Sidefits implements all reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access by third parties to saved details.
However, Sidefits assumes no liability for the interception, alteration, or misuse of the information you provide other users by other users. The user explicitly consents that Sidefits may provide non-personally identifiable information to other parties for marketing, advertising or other uses.

IV Facebook Integration

The Sidefits application integrates different connection to the social network Facebook, 1601 South California Avenue, Palo Alto, CA 94304, USA. They are used to share activities on Facebook and to get Facebook information about Sidefits' application users. An overview of Facebook integrations and social plugins can be found here: http://developers.facebook.com/docs/plugins/. When users enable the Facebook connection, the Facebook integration establishes a connection between a user and the Facebook server. Thereby, Sidefits receives information about the user. This information will be treated as stated in section III. When users invite friends via Facebook, the Facebook integration establishes a connection between a user and the Facebook server. That allows the user to gain information about his or her friends via the Sidefits application. Sidefits as the provider of the Sidefits application does not receive any of this information. When users share their performances on Facebook and when they invite friends via Facebook, the Facebook integration establishes a connection between a user and the Facebook server. Thereby, the user links information of his Sidefits account to his or her Facebook account. That allows Facebook to correlate a user's usage of the Sidefits application with the corresponding users Facebook account. Sidefits as the provider of the Sidefits application does not have any knowledge of the future data usage by Facebook. Users can find more information on that issue in the privacy policy of Facebook at: http://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/. Users have to actively enable the Facebook connection by signing in in order to be able to share content from the Sidefits application on Facebook and invite friends via Facebook. If users do not want Facebook to gain information on their Sidefits application usage or if they don't want Sidefits to gain information about them via Facebook, they shall leave the Facebook integration disabled and register with email. By activating the Facebook connection, the user explicitly consents to the information exchange described in this section.

V Cookies

Sidefits and other members of our group of companies that provide us with services as well as our external service providers may place cookies or web beacons onto your computer when you visit the Sidefits website. Sidefits uses cookies; cookies are small files that are stored on your hard drive. They make navigation easier. Cookies also help us with identification. We use these cookies and web beacons to monitor your preferences and present the Sidefits website in the best possible way, to recognize returning users, to measure user traffic and activities during visits to the Sidefits website, to monitor and improve our service and to guard against fraud. We also use cookies to individualize our service, content, advertising and product line. We may use the services of external advertising companies which may place cookies in order to allow them to collect and use anonymous data concerning your visit to the Sidefits website. You can block cookies at any time by changing your browser settings. However, this might mean that we are unable to provide certain services. In order to register as a member of Sidefits your browser must be set to accept cookies.
